About the Artist

Elodie Montoro is an Italian artist, sculptress and mosaicist currently based in London. The artist’s approach to her craft is contemplative and skillfully balanced. She produces harmonious work that taps into femininity. With such a diverse approach to the artistic mediums, Elodie never fails to express herself, her soul and her essence. The artist studied Mosaic at Salvelli Vatican Mosaic’s School and then Painting and Sculpture at both Central Saint Martin’s and Kensington College of Art, London. Elodie has exhibited at the Contemporary Art Exhibition in Marylebone, London and extensively throughout Italy.
